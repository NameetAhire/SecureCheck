Our Story at SecureCheck

Helping you stay safe online by spotting data breaches before they cause trouble

Why We Started SecureCheck

We created SecureCheck because we know how scary it feels when your personal information gets exposed online. We've been there ourselves, and we wanted to build something that helps everyone stay one step ahead of cyber threats.

What We Actually Do

Think of us as your personal online security guard. We search through huge collections of data from past security breaches to see if your email address has ever been caught up in one of these incidents.

We help you understand where your information might be floating around online, so you can take control and protect your accounts before something bad happens.

Why This Matters to You

Honestly, data breaches happen way more often than people realize. Every year, millions of people's information gets exposed without them even knowing about it.

Finding out if your email has been part of a breach is like getting an early warning system. It lets you fix things before someone tries to misuse your information.

Questions We Get Asked A Lot

Here are answers to some common things people wonder about

How do you protect my privacy?

This is probably our most important question! When you check your email with us, we only use it to search through our breach databases. We don't keep your email address stored away for later, and we definitely don't share it with anyone else. Your trust means everything to us.

What should I do if my email shows up in a breach?

First, don't panic! We'll give you clear steps to follow. Usually this means changing passwords for any important accounts that use that email, turning on two-factor authentication if you can, and keeping an eye on your accounts for anything suspicious. We break it down so it's not overwhelming.

How often do you update your information?

We're always adding new breach data as it becomes available. Our own database gets updated whenever we find new, verified information, and we regularly check public sources too. We want to make sure you're getting the most current picture of your online security.

Can companies use SecureCheck for their team?

Absolutely! We work with businesses who want to check if any of their employees' emails have been compromised. We offer special access and tools that make it easy for companies to keep their whole team secure. Just reach out to us and we'll help you get set up.
